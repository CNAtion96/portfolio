About Me

My name is Chris Adkins, I’m a Front-End Developer and graduate of The Iron Yard Cincinnati. Basically, I get paid to be the best nerd I can be. I am a very driven person with a desire to learn.

I started teaching myself how to program five years ago as a hobby. I started out learning Python and went on to learn basic Java at Marshall University. By dabbling in HTML and CSS in my free time I found a love for web technologies that I just didn’t have for software. I went on to join The Iron Yard Cincinnati’s twelve-week Front-End bootcamp. It was an intense program, but that intensity only served to further my passion of working with the web.

When I don’t have my face buried in my laptop working on something new you’ll probably find me with my nose buried in the pages of a new book or rereading the Harry Potter series for the umpteenth time.
